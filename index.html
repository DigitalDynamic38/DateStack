About DATESTACK

I’m a 1st-year CSE BTech student who created DATESTACK to help students know important events clearly and quickly.

DATESTACK organizes key dates category-wise and uses quizzes to make remembering easier and more fun.

“Knowing is different from learning; we have to know everything, learning is secondary.”

This site is for anyone who wants to explore essential events and build solid knowledge.
